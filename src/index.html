Great Tea, Freshly Presented

Embark on a regal journey with Gong Cha, where the art of tea meets imperial excellence. In Chinese tradition, "gong cha" signifies the offering of the finest tea to the emperor - a testament to unparalleled quality and the highest standards. Since our establishment in 2006, Gong Cha has captivated tea enthusiasts with our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our success story, rooted in Taiwan, blossoms from the harmonious blend of time-honored techniques and innovative flavors. We've earned the admiration of our cherished customers through genuine word-of-mouth praise and our signature personalized service.
